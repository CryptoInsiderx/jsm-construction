# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Homeowners and commercial property owners in Windsor, Ontario, Canada. They are vetting a local contractor before calling or submitting an inquiry. They arrive with a specific project in mind (basement, bathroom, kitchen, deck) and need to quickly decide if JSM is trustworthy and capable before making contact. Mobile-first audience browsing after work hours.

## Product Purpose

Generate leads for JSM Construction Limited — primarily phone calls to 519-980-8818 and secondary inquiry form submissions. Build credibility for a family-run, owner-operated construction company that competes on trust and quality, not price. Director Jagjit Singh Mann leads the business personally.

## Brand Personality

Established, Honest, Reliable. A company that shows up, does the work, and stands behind it. Not a startup, not a franchise — a real Windsor contractor with roots in the community.

## Anti-references

- Generic contractor template sites: blue gradient heroes, stock hard-hat photography, cheesy "We give 110%!" taglines, phone numbers plastered everywhere in red, Comic Sans or Impact-adjacent fonts.
- Busy trade sites: low contrast, clip-art icons, cluttered navigation, 10 different CTAs competing at once.

## Design Principles

1. **Trust through substance** — Every section earns credibility with specifics: real reviews, real services, real contact info. No filler copy.
2. **Clarity before cleverness** — The phone number and inquiry form are always reachable. No buried CTAs.
3. **Trade confidence without trappings** — Feels like a serious, capable company, not a luxury brand and not a cheap flyer.
4. **Owner-led warmth** — This is Jagjit Singh Mann's company. The voice is direct and personal, not corporate.
5. **One decision at a time** — Guide visitors from "can they do what I need?" to "I should call them" without overwhelming choices.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. High contrast (the brand crimson on dark or light must meet 4.5:1). No reliance on color alone for meaning. Form must be keyboard-navigable.
